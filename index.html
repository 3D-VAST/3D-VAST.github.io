2. Topic

As large-scale 3D scene modeling becomes increasingly important for applications such as urban planning, robotics, autonomous navigation, and virtual simulations...

Technical Challenges

  • Sparse and incomplete views
  • Visual ambiguities across altitudes
  • Spatio-temporal inconsistencies
  • Dynamic scene changes

Workshop Topics

  1. Cross-altitude feature matching and registration
  2. View synthesis from sparse/heterogeneous data
  3. Sparse-view 3D reconstruction
  4. Generative approaches for view completion
  5. Datasets and benchmarks
  6. Real-world applications in urban planning
